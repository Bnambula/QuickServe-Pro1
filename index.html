📜 Terms & Conditions — QuickServe Pro
Effective 2025 · Governed by laws of Uganda
QuickServe Pro is a marketplace facilitator — NOT an employer or direct service provider.

1. Nature of Platform

We act solely as an intermediary. We do not employ providers or deliver services directly.

2. Real Identity Required

All users must register with their real legal identity per NIN. False information = immediate permanent ban.

3. Payments

Via MoMo or Airtel Money held in escrow. Commission of 10–25% deducted per transaction.

4. Liability Disclaimer

QuickServe Pro is NOT liable for property damage, theft, poor service, or personal injury.

5. Governing Law

Governed by the laws of Uganda. Disputes resolved through platform process or Ugandan courts.

👷 Worker Agreement
You are NOT an employee of QuickServe Pro. You are an independent contractor.

1. Independent Status

No employment relationship. You set your own schedule and accept/reject jobs freely.

2. Mandatory Verification

Valid NIN, legal name, live selfie, OTP phone required. Fake info = permanent ban.

3. Physical ID Confirmation

Before every job you must show your National ID to the customer for confirmation.

4. Conduct Standards

Respect customers, don't steal or damage property, no substances, all transactions on-platform.

5. Risk Score & Termination

2 complaints = warning. 3–4 = suspension. 5+ = permanent ban. Serious offences = police report.

🧑‍💼 Customer Agreement

1. Platform Role

QuickServe Pro connects you with independent providers. Platform not responsible for delivery quality.

2. Verify Provider ID

Before work begins, ask the provider to show their National ID and confirm it matches the app profile.

For your safety — verify the provider's ID before work begins.

3. Payment Obligations

Pay through the platform only. Late cancellations may incur a penalty fee.

4. Safe Environment

Provide a safe, respectful environment. Harassment results in immediate account termination.

🔒 Privacy Policy

1. Data Collected

Full name, NIN, ID photo, selfie, phone, location (during jobs), job history, payment records, chat logs, device/IP data.

2. How We Use It

Verification, matching, payment processing, dispute resolution, fraud prevention. We NEVER sell your data.

3. Your Rights

Request access, correction, or deletion at any time. Contact: privacy@quickservepro.ug

⚖️ Dispute Resolution

Step 1: Report

Click Report Issue → Select type → Submit. Done in under 10 seconds.

Step 2: Freeze & Collect

Payment frozen instantly. Chat logs, GPS, photos, timestamps auto-collected.

Step 3: Review (24hrs)

Admin reviews all evidence and may contact both parties for additional information.

Step 4: Decision & Appeal

Full refund, partial refund, or payment release. One appeal within 48 hours. Final decision is binding.
